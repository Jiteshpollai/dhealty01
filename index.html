Who We Are

A Hybrid Organization
Built for Impact

BBO operates as both a Trust/NGO and a Private Limited company — unlocking the best of both worlds.

Brindavan Bhawan Organization (BBO) is headquartered in Bhubaneswar, Odisha. Our hybrid model allows us to access grants, CSR funding, and donations through our NGO arm while generating sustainable revenue through paid healthcare services.

We believe health, disability care, and elder welfare should not be mutually exclusive from financial sustainability. BBO proves that doing good and building good business go hand in hand.

Starting with DhealthyDiet as our cash-flow engine, we will systematically fund and launch our Down Syndrome Care Center and Old Age Home over 12 months.

Execution Plan

From Idea to Empire
— Step by Step

A phased rollout designed to minimize risk, validate early, and scale with confidence.

🚀
Phase 1
Foundation
0 – 3 Months
  • Register NGO/Trust (Brindavan Bhawan Organization)
  • Register company (DhealthyDiet)
  • Build brand identity: logo, website, GMB, YouTube
  • Launch DhealthyDiet — online first
  • Free webinar + first 50 clients discounted
📈
Phase 2
Validation
3 – 6 Months
  • Open small physical clinic in Bhubaneswar
  • Hire 1–2 experienced dieticians
  • Begin paid consultations & monthly diet plans
  • Build hospital, gym & diagnostic center partnerships
  • Launch corporate wellness campaign

Expansion — Down Syndrome Center

  • Launch Down Syndrome Day Care Center

Reality Check

Risks & Smart Strategy

Big visions need honest assessments. Here's what to avoid and what to do instead.

❌ Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Starting all 3 verticals simultaneously
  • Hiring too many people too early
  • Overspending on infrastructure in Phase 1
  • Skipping the online-first validation step

✅ The Smart Strategy

  • Start with DhealthyDiet as the cash-flow engine
  • Use DhealthyDiet profits to fund the other two verticals
  • Keep infrastructure minimal in Phase 1 & 2
  • Validate online before opening a physical clinic
